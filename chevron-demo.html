Chevron Style Options

Option 1: Right-rotating chevron (current implementation)
Click to expand/collapse
This is the current implementation. Chevron points right when closed, rotates 90° to point down when open.
Option 2: Down/up swap (no rotation)
Click to expand/collapse
Points down (∨) when closed, up (∧) when open. No rotation animation.
Option 3: Plus/minus
Click to expand/collapse
Shows + when closed, − when open. Clean and minimalist.
Option 4: macOS Finder style triangle
Click to expand/collapse
Triangle points right (▸) when closed, down (▾) when open. Familiar macOS pattern.
Option 5: Subtle down chevron (opacity change)
Click to expand/collapse
Always points down, but opacity changes. Closed = subtle (50%), open = full opacity.
